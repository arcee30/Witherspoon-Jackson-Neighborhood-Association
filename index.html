Who We Are

Welcome to the official website of the Witherspoon-Jackson Neighborhood Association (WJNA), the heart and soul of Princeton, New Jersey's rich African American heritage. Nestled within the vibrant town, Witherspoon-Jackson stands as the 20th historic district, exuding a remarkable sense of community, culture, and resilience. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate this cherished heritage while fostering a spirit of unity and progress. Through various initiatives, events, and collaborations, we strive to honor the past, empower the present, and shape a bright future for all residents and visitors alike.

"Tell somebody, bring somebody... we are building community, try not to come alone."

While the WJNA conducts meetings for the benefit of residents and businesses in the WJ neighborhood,
all interested parties are encouraged and welcome to attend this and all subsequent meetings on topics
of interest to all neighborhoods and the community at large.
